## Problem

Expanded itinerary cards on mobile use very large typography and spacing. The first card shows only tips before the fold and hides food/suggested activity context lower down.

## Proposed fix

Create a mobile-specific day detail layout with compact section headers, tighter spacing, and collapsed sub-sections for Tips, Food, Transport, Accommodation, and Activities.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] First expanded day shows more than one detail section above the fold.
