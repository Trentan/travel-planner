## Problem

The mobile menu sheet is a dense grid of equally-sized buttons. It's hard to distinguish between configuration, functional actions, and data actions.

## Before

Screenshot: [mde-04-menu-open.png](../screenshots/before/mde-04-menu-open.png)

Mode: MDE

Callout: The grid layout makes every action look the same.

## Proposed Fix

Reorganize the mobile menu into sections with small headers. Use different button styles for primary vs secondary actions.

Proposal: [WI-030-proposal.png](../screenshots/after/WI-030-proposal.png)

## After

Latest review screenshot: [WI-030-after.png](../screenshots/after/WI-030-after.png)

The mobile menu is grouped into "Trip settings", "Planning tools", "Export & files", and "Maintenance", with primary actions visually separated from secondary actions.
