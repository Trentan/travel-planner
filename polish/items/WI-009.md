## Problem

The map tab draws a simplified SVG world map rather than an interactive map. It also reports unmapped trip cities including itinerary locations that matter to this real trip.

## Proposed fix

Replace the SVG approximation with Leaflet or another lightweight tile map, and use real coordinates from the city database for all trip cities.

## After (proposed state description)

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Map supports pan and zoom.
- [ ] No real trip destination is listed as unmapped when coordinates exist or can be stored.
- [ ] Regression check passes: `node scripts/regression-city-nav.js`
