## Problem

Costs are sometimes displayed as raw numbers or strings without consistent formatting. Some places show `$`, others don't.

## Before

Screenshot: [mde-07-budget.png](../screenshots/before/mde-07-budget.png)

Mode: MDE

Callout: Check total costs in day cards vs budget tab.

## Proposed Fix

Centralize currency formatting in `js/utils.js` with `formatCurrency(amount)`.

Proposal: [WI-032-proposal.png](../screenshots/after/WI-032-proposal.png)

## After

Latest review screenshot: [WI-032-after.png](../screenshots/after/WI-032-after.png)

## How Implemented

1. Added shared `parseCurrencyAmount` and `formatCurrency` helpers in `js/utils.js`.
2. Reused the helper across day totals, suggested activity estimates, accommodation costs, transport costs, mobile status/cost meta, and budget totals.
3. Kept editable cost values parseable by formatting the numeric portion separately from the visible currency symbol where needed.
