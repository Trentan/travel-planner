## Problem

Costs are sometimes displayed as raw numbers or strings without consistent formatting. Some places show `$`, others don't.

## Before (current state)

> Screenshot: `../screenshots/before/mde-07-budget.png`
> Mode: MDE
> Callout: Check total costs in day cards vs budget tab.

## Proposed fix

Centralize currency formatting in `js/utils.js` (e.g., `formatCurrency(amount)`).

**Mockup Creation:**
If the change is visual, you MUST create a visual mockup (Option A or B in Step 6) and link it here.

## After (proposed state description)

All cost displays show a consistent currency symbol, two decimal places where appropriate, and thousands separators.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] All cost fields formatted via shared helper.
- [ ] Regression check passes: `node scripts/regression-city-nav.js`

## How to implement

1. Define `formatCurrency` in `js/utils.js`.
2. Audit all tabs for cost rendering and replace with helper.
