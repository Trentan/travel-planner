## Problem

On mobile, the combination of the sticky menu bar, tab navigation, and city navigation consumes nearly 30-40% of the vertical viewport. This leaves very little room for viewing itinerary cards or budget tables.

## Before

Screenshot: [mde-01-home.png](../screenshots/before/mde-01-home.png)

Mode: MDE

Callout: Notice how little content is visible below the navigation stack.

## Proposed Fix

Replace the persistent mobile top menu bar with a compact navigation row. Keep the tab strip and city strip visible, integrate a single menu button into the tab strip, and move file/status plus compact mode controls into the mobile menu sheet.

Proposal: [WI-028-proposal.png](../screenshots/after/WI-028-proposal.png)

## How Implemented

1. Added a mobile-only menu button to the tab navigation in `index.html`.
2. Moved mobile trip file/status context into the menu sheet and synchronized it from the existing status nodes in `js/ui.js`.
3. Hid the persistent top app menu on mobile and styled the integrated nav/menu control in `style.css`.
