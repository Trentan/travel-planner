## Problem

Budget values render as raw decimals such as `$3465.1`, `$110.6`, and `$217.6`. This looks accidental for travel budgeting and reduces trust in totals.

## Proposed fix

Centralize currency formatting in `buildBudgetTab()` with `Intl.NumberFormat`, using zero or two decimals consistently depending on whether cents are meaningful.
